# TextBack

**Human-paced AI texting.**  
Not everyone replies right away. That's the point.

A monthly service that lets you text AI personas — your coach, best friend, therapist, practice crush, competitor, or steady listener — with **realistic delayed responses** (hours, not seconds).

## The Idea

Instant AI replies feel fake. Real conversations have rhythm, pauses, and serendipity.

TextBack recreates that feeling. Pick a persona, get a real phone number, and text them like a normal person. Responses arrive in a natural, randomized window — 4 to 20 hours depending on the persona — making the conversation feel authentic and valuable.

## Personas

- **The Coach** — Holds you accountable, pushes back on excuses
- **The Friend** — Listens without fixing, makes you feel heard
- **The Practice Run** — Safe space to rehearse tough messages (crush, boss, family)
- **The Adversary** — Stress-tests your ideas like a competitor or skeptic would
- **The Steady** — Calm, consistent voice for processing heavy thoughts

## Key Features

- No app required — works directly in your native Messages
- Realistic human-like response timing (randomized)
- Full conversation memory and context across threads
- Shared context across all personas (on higher tier)
- Unlimited texting

## Pricing

- **One Person** — $15/month  
- **The Full Roster** — $25/month (all 5 personas)

## How It Works

1. Choose your persona(s)
2. Get assigned a phone number
3. Save it in your contacts and text normally
4. Receive thoughtful replies in hours

## Tech

This site is a single `index.html` file. Just:

1. Rename `textback-landing.html` → `index.html`
2. Push to GitHub
3. Import the repo in [Vercel](https://vercel.com)

Vercel will auto-deploy it as a static site.
